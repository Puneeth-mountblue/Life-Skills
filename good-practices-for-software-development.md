# Good Practices for Software Development

## Question 1

Which point(s) were new to you?

* Using public channels over private DMs for better visibility
* Sharing problems with screenshots, gists, or sandboxes instead of plain text
* Joining meetings early to build rapport
* Managing food and exercise as part of productivity
* Blocking social media during work hours to protect focus

## Question 2

Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress?

* Over-communication

  * Proactively share delays, blockers, and updates early

* Asking better questions

  * Clearly explain the issue, context, and what I already tried

* Deep focus during work

  * Silence phone, block distractions, and time-box focused work sessions

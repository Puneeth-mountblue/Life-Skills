# Focus Management

## 1. What is Deep Work

Deep Work is focused, distraction-free work on cognitively demanding tasks that produce high-value results.

## 2. Optimal Duration for Deep Work

* Deep work sessions should be limited to 1–2 hours for maximum focus.
* Multiple sessions can be done in a day with breaks in between.

## 3. Are Deadlines Good for Productivity

* Deadlines can increase focus and motivate completion.
* Short, challenging deadlines push performance.

## 4. Summary of Deep Work Book

### How to Do Deep Work Properly

* Work in distraction-free blocks
* Schedule deep work sessions
* Embrace boredom, avoid constant switching tasks
* Focus on high-value tasks
* Limit social media and shallow work

### Implementing Principles Daily

* Allocate 1–2 hours of uninterrupted work
* Turn off notifications during deep work
* Batch shallow tasks for other times
* Use “one-task at a time” approach

## 5. Dangers of Social Media

* Wastes time
* Reduces focus and attention span
* Increases stress and anxiety
* Promotes comparison and negative emotions
* Encourages shallow thinking

## 6. Creating Diffused Mode

* Meditation
* Walking without digital distractions
* Standing or sitting in silence
* Lying down or Shavasana
* Focused physical chores like cleaning

## 7. Getting Started on Difficult Tasks

* Work for just 30 seconds on the task
* Take a short break
* Remind yourself you are capable
* Gradually increase working time
* Build proof of ability to shift mindset and overcome procrastination

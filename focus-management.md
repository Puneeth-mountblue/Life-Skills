# **1. What Is Deep Work?**

* Deep Work is the ability to focus without distraction
* It involves working on challenging, meaningful tasks
* It allows you to produce high-quality results in less time
* It requires full concentration and no multitasking
* It leads to mastery, creativity, and better productivity

---

# **2. How to Do Deep Work Properly (According to the Author)**

* Schedule deep work sessions intentionally
* Remove all distractions (especially digital devices)
* Work in blocks of focused, uninterrupted time
* Prioritize important tasks and do them first
* Create a ritual or routine before starting
* Track the time spent in deep work
* End each session with a clear stopping point

---

# **3. How You Can Use These Principles in Daily Life**

* Set specific deep work hours every day
* Keep your phone away or on silent during work
* Work in quiet spaces
* Use a timer (Pomodoro style or longer blocks)
* Plan 2–3 important tasks each day
* Avoid multitasking and context switching
* Have a shutdown routine at the end of the day

---

# **4. Dangers of Social Media (Brief)**

* Reduces attention span
* Increases distraction and breaks focus
* Can cause stress, anxiety, and comparison
* Creates addiction through constant dopamine hits
* Steals time from meaningful work
* Weakens ability to concentrate deeply
* Encourages shallow thinking

---

# **5. Intentionally Creating Diffused Mode**

Activities that help diffused thinking:

* Meditation
* Walking without phone
* Standing or sitting in silence
* Lying down quietly (Shavasana)
* Doing simple chores with full presence

---

# **6. Getting Started on Difficult Tasks (Overcoming Procrastination)**


* Work for **30 seconds** to break the resistance
* Take a short break
* Remind yourself: “I am capable; this emotion is temporary”
* Repeat the cycle and slowly increase work time
* Build small proofs of success to build confidence
* Shift identity from “I avoid hard tasks” to “I handle hard tasks”
